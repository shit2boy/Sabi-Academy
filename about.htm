Who we are

Team Achievers comprises a group of young tech enthusiasts driven to causing disruptive change in Nigeria's education sector. This is rooted in the belief that an excellent education is the catalyst for a nation's growth and development.

Our Vision

To utilize technology as a resource tool in providing quality education to children living in low-income communities. So as to ensure holistic growth through affordable quality education regardless of their geographical location.

Our Intervention

Sabi Academy leverages on technology to deliver quality education to children living in low-income communities. It is a learning environment that facilitates access to an excellent education for children at the primary school level. We engineer this process by engaging visual, audio-visual and Kinesthetic approach in content delivery to the learners learning on our platform. All learning content is geared towards facilitating learning of 21st century skills which comprises critical thinking, creativity, collaboration and communication. Sabi Academy uniquely translates all learning content to Nigeria's three major ethnic languages which are Igbo,Yoruba and Hausa.
